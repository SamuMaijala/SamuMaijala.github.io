Why build a site like this?

I’m Samu, a 22-year-old cybersecurity student at Laurea University. Building this website and completing the Fundamentals of Web Development course have marked my first steps into the world of web development. This site showcases the skills I’ve acquired throughout the course, focusing on the ones I feel most confident in utilizing as I believe I understand them best.

I have chosen to create a short directory as my project because it’s something I’ve needed for a while and serves a practical purpose.

My background

As mentioned before, I am a 22-year-old cybersecurity student based in Espoo, Finland. I am currently in my first year at university, studying a field in which I have a somewhat limited background, having previously focused on economics during my time at Vaasa University. Over the past year, I’ve been enthusiastically diving into web development and programming, finding it both fascinating and highly rewarding. I hope this website is just the first of many projects to come!

Site structure

This site is built upon the foundation of the W3.CSS Parallax Template, which intrigued me with its clean, grey, and straightforward structure. The template’s influence is most evident on the homepage and this information page, where I’ve customized the design to reflect my preferences. In contrast, the FAQ and directory pages follow a more rigid and simplified structure to ensure the information is presented as clearly as possible. The site also incorporates publicly available media and AI-generated background images to enhance its visual appeal.
